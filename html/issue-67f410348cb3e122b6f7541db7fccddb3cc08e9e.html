Introduce a notation for 'a range of objects'

« cadabra project page

When declaring large sets of indices it would be useful to have a notation which automatically takes care of declaring a range of them, e.g.

{a..z}::Indices.

or

{\alpha..\zeta}::Indices.

Since the range operator already exists this should not be too hard.
